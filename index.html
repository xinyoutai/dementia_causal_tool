Domain-Scoring Matrix for Dementia Risk Factors

Risk Factor

Enter the risk factor you are assessing.

Rating Levels

Each domain will be rated on four levels:

Strong Criteria met
Moderate Partially met
Weak Evidence gap
Contradictory Evidence against

How It Works

You will assess five evidence domains (A–E) by ticking sub-criteria and selecting a rating for each. The tool will then map your domain profile to an overall evidence grade (I–IV or Disease Marker) and generate a summary statement.
